SOME OF MY FAVOURITE FOODS

BROWNIE

Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. Brownies often, but not always, have a glossy "skin" on their upper crust.

SWISS ROLL

In spite of the name "Swiss roll", the cake is believed to have originated elsewhere in Central Europe, possibly Austria or Slovenia.

CHOCOLATE PAN CAKE

Very soft and warm, a sweet wheatish flavor. With maple syrup and butter it's almost like candy. Pancakes are sweet, soft, and similar to cake-- but flat.
